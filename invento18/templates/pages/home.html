Invento 2020 is the annual multifest of Government Engineering College Palakkad, a ground breaking amalgamation of science, art and technology. It is a union of its subsets, Invento, the flagship technical extravaganza of GEC Palakkad and Saptha, an Arts Festival. Attended by a plethora of people and spread over a course of 4 days, this year commemorates the 6th series of this premier festivity that vies to surpass it's predecessors in terms of grandeur and innovation. With the underlying theme, 'From Stone Age to Future' being focused upon, Invento 2020 is envisaged to shift paradigms and traverse beyond the limits of impossible horizons.
